# The Terminal Universe Manifesto

Terminal emulators are not just tools.

They are environments of thought.

They are where code becomes action, where systems become visible, and where abstraction meets reality.

## We believe

The diversity of terminal emulators reflects different philosophies of interaction, simplicity, performance, control, and evolution.

## We reject

The idea that terminals are interchangeable. Each one encodes decisions: what matters, what is ignored, and what is made possible.

## We build

A map of this space. Not to rank. Not to judge. But to understand.

## We embrace

A hybrid approach: rigorous enough to be reproducible, intuitive enough to be explored.
